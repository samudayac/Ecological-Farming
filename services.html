Our Services


Here are some services that the Department of Agriculture could offer through this web-based application to promote ecological farming and support agricultural stakeholders:

  1. Information on organic farming practices : Provide detailed information on organic farming techniques, including crop rotation, composting, natural pest control methods, and organic soil management.

  2. Training and workshops : Offer online training sessions, workshops, and webinars on organic farming practices, sustainable agriculture, and ecological farming methods.

  3. Access to resources : Provide access to resources such as organic farming manuals, guides, research papers, and case studies to help farmers implement organic farming practices effectively.

  4. Consultation services : Offer online consultation services where farmers can seek advice and guidance from agricultural experts on transitioning to organic farming methods and addressing specific challenges.

  5. Certification assistance : Guide farmers through the process of obtaining organic certification, including providing information on certification requirements, procedures, and standards.

  6. Marketplace for organic products : Create an online marketplace where farmers can sell their organic produce directly to consumers, thereby facilitating the marketing and sale of organic products.

  7. Community forums and networking : Establish online forums and networking platforms where farmers can connect with each other, share experiences, ask questions, and exchange ideas related to organic farming.

  8. Weather and crop advisory : Provide weather forecasts, crop advisory services, and pest alerts to help farmers make informed decisions about their farming practices.

By offering these services through this web-based application, the Department of Agriculture can effectively promote ecological farming practices, raise awareness among agricultural stakeholders, and support the growth of the organic farming sector in Sri Lanka.
