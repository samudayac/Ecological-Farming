About Us


The Department of Agriculture (DOA) functions under the Ministry of Agriculture and is one of the largest government departments with a high profile community of agricultural scientists and a network of institutions covering different agro ecological regions island wide.



Our Mission:


Our goal at the DOA is to promote sustainable agricultural development in Sri Lanka. This is our clear and unwavering aim. Our commitment is in our ongoing research and dissemination of new agriculture technologies to ensure the food and nutrition security of the country. Our goal is to build a future in which ecological principles and agriculture coexist. Our vision goes beyond the present.



Our contribution to ecological farming:


Leading the charge in the transition to sustainable agriculture is the DOA. We understand how crucial it is to use ethical agricultural methods that preserve future generations' health in addition to supplying the requirements of the present. Our dedication is centered on:


  • Environmental Protection: We recognize that soil, water, and climate play critical roles in supporting ecosystems and agriculture, so we place a high priority on protecting them.

  • Biodiversity Promotion: We actively support biodiversity through our projects because we recognize its importance in building resilient and thriving agricultural landscapes.

  • Chemical-Free and Non-GMO Practices: We support ecological farming practices by avoiding using chemicals or genetically modified organisms to contaminate the ecosystem. Our method guarantees healthful, clean food production and healthy farming.



Why should you join us as a stakeholder?


To achieve globally competitive production, processing and marketing enterprises through socially acceptable, innovative and commercially-oriented agriculture, through sustainable management of natural resources of the country
